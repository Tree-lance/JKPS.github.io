Our Mission

Our mission is to work with Landlords and Estate Agents to ensure that we all reach our goals. What seperates us from our competitors is that Landlords in particular will benefit from legal advice on housing issues should they require. We take on properties and manage them on behalf of Landlords so they no longer have to deal with tenants direct. Most importantly we allow Landlords to have the peace of mind of knowing that they will receive a fixed rent with no void periods. Estate Agents would also benefit from using us, by having their properties taken off their rota allowing them to reach their daily or weekly targets. How It Works

1.Discussion

We engage in detailed conversations with the Landlord or Estate Agent to discover whether our services is something they are interested in or whether whatwe do is suitable to their needs. We would explain in detail what we do, how we do it and what we can offer them. Any offer made would be based on a detailed breakdown of the Landlords NET rent after cost.

2.Agreement

Following our intial discussions, if the Landlord or Estate Agents are happy to continue, we will put forward an offer to take on their property under either a Corperate Let Agreement or a Management Contract. Any agreement reached would be for a fixed period of up to 5 years and include a break clause. We believe that being honest and open is the key to any worthwhile relationship and therefore the break clause allows either party to leave without any consequences.

3.Management of Property

Once an agreement has been reached, we will manage the property at no additional cost and in doing so, as part of the agreement we will either rent the rooms out to professional individuals or the property as a whole. Regardless of how the property is rented out the Landlord will receive the agreed rental income whilst having the peace of mind knowiing that the property will be professionally managed with no voids.

4.Landlords Guaranteed Rent

Once the property is in our possession, the Landlord can sit back and relax knowing that they will receive the agreed rental income without any void periods. An addtional part of our service to Landlords is that they would also benefit from my personal experience of being a Chartered Legal Executive who specialises in Housing Matters. We would provide the Landlord with legal advice on any Housing issues. We believe that this is what seperates us from our competitors.
